About Us

With the motto of serving the people, Chatrapati Multistate Co-operative Credit Society Ltd. was founded in 2014, by Shri. Santosh (Nana) Bhandari. The idea to empower people was ably supported by a group of like-minded individuals. The first step of empowerment was to give financial support to the ambitious yet needy people, who believed in passionately following their dreams. Over the years, the passion, dedication, and vision of Shri. Santosh Bhandari has diversified the operations of Chatrapati Multistate. Today, the family is moving ahead with 21 branches and more than 1,00,000 satisfied account holders in a very short period where CMCS continues to support more and more dreams, across the rural-urban region of India.

Success Journey

Our journey that started in Beed has reached across many cities and we tried to positively impact the lives of thousands and lakhs of individuals, it has been able to connect with. In just six years, we have established 21 branches in 8 districts & 2 states. Our Services




  Current Account

It is used by individuals, businesses, etc who have regular transactions with the bank. We help you make rapid progress in your business & personal life. 

 Fix Deposit (FD)

FD's offer greater returns on the principal invested when compared to the returns generated from a regular savings account. With us let your funds grow safely. 

 Recurring deposit (RD)

RD's, help people with regular incomes to deposit a fixed amount every month into their recurring deposit account and earn interest at the rate applicable to fixed deposits.
